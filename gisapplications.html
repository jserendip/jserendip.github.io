GIS is a broad field

Within the perview of GIS there are many related fields, such as: Geovisualization, Spatial Analysis, Cartography, Surveying, Remote Sensing, Spectral Analysis and more!

ESRI has long been a leader in the field, with powerful tools for most of these applications. Visit them at:esri.com and you will see they place an emphasis on sustainability. This is because most modern land use programs require GIS technology both for efficiencies, and to help chart potential impacts, both financial and ecological.
